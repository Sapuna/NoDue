GCIT Due-System

GCIT due-system refers to a policy in which students are not allowed to graduate or leave the college until they have cleared all outstanding dues owed to the college.

Under a no-due system, students are required to settle all outstanding dues related to any departments and any other expenses they may have incurred during their time at the college.

Failure to clear these dues may result in the student being denied their departure or official transcripts.



Instructions

Log in to Dues Clearance System using the CC User ID and Password.

If you are Not Graduating; please do not use the Dues Clearance System.

Apply to all the department using the "Apply" button on the homepage.

Please ensure that the No Dues Status column in every department's column reads "confirmed." If any due appears in one of these columns, kindly resolve it with the respective department.

Once the columns of all departments shows as "confirmed", concerned department will clear the dues, a notification will be send to you.
